## Statement

No production trading claim is made by this paper pack. The released material is a research and shadow-protocol evidence package.

## Financial / Operational Boundary

- The official object is a daily shadow target, not an executed live trading account.
- Negative target weights are long-short shadow accounting, not proof of literal A-share short execution.
- No real slippage, fill, broker reconciliation, or capacity claim is made.
- Diagnostic/oracle variants are not official selection rules.
